Dr. Rajat Chauhan is a Sports-Exercise Medicine & Musculoskeletal Pain Physician at Back 2 Fitness, Running Programs Mentor, and passionate advocate for movement as medicine.

With over two decades of experience, he helps people achieve their impossible — whether recovering from chronic back pain or completing ultra-distance runs. His philosophy: the body is designed to move, and movement heals.

He is the founder of La Ultra – The High, one of the world's most challenging ultramarathons set in Ladakh, and has authored three groundbreaking books on pain and movement.

La Ultra – The High is one of the world's most gruelling ultramarathons, set amidst the majestic Himalayas of Ladakh. Founded by Dr. Rajat Chauhan, this race pushes participants through extremes of altitude, terrain, and human endurance.

With distances of 55km, 111km, and 222km, it is a race that tests not just the body, but the mind and spirit — embodying Dr. Chauhan's belief that impossible is a choice, not a destination.
