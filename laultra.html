La Ultra — The High

Where Limits Go to Die

One of the world's most gruelling ultramarathons, set amidst the majestic Himalayas of Ladakh.

222kmMax Distance
5,400mPeak Altitude
3+Mountain Passes
72hrsTime Limit

La Ultra – The High pushes participants through extremes of altitude, terrain, and human endurance. Founded by Dr. Rajat Chauhan in 2009, the race has become a global pilgrimage for the world's toughest ultra-runners.

With distances of 55km, 111km, and 222km, it is a race that tests not just the body, but the mind and spirit — embodying Dr. Chauhan's belief that impossible is a choice, not a destination.
