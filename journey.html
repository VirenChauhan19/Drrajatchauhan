Journey

Milestones in Motion

Two decades of building sports medicine, running culture, and unforgettable races across India.

2006

Founding Head — Sports & Exercise Medicine

Established the first dedicated Sports & Exercise Medicine department at Manipal Hospital, Bangalore — bringing modern sports medicine to mainstream Indian healthcare.

2007 – 2011

ICC Cricket World Cup Faculty

Served as faculty member providing sports medicine expertise to elite cricketers across the World Cup cycles.

2009

Founded La Ultra — The High

Created one of the world's toughest ultramarathons in the Himalayas of Ladakh. From a wild idea to a global pilgrimage for ultra-runners.

2014 – 2016

Founder Director — Ashoka University

Built and led the sports & wellness division at one of India's most ambitious liberal arts universities.

2014 – 2017

Principal Running Consultant — Adidas India

Served as chief technical running consultant for Adidas India, shaping how a generation learned to run.

Present

Back 2 Fitness & Global Advocacy

Transforming lives through the Back 2 Fitness clinic, books, his Hindustan Times column, and the Run 2 Fly program with SwaTaleem.
