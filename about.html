Dr. Rajat Chauhan is a Sports-Exercise Medicine & Musculoskeletal Pain Physician at Back 2 Fitness, Running Programs Mentor, and a passionate advocate for movement as medicine.

With over two decades of experience, he helps people achieve their impossible — whether recovering from chronic back pain or completing ultra-distance runs. His philosophy is simple: the body is designed to move, and movement heals.

He is the founder of La Ultra – The High, one of the world's most challenging ultramarathons set in the Himalayas of Ladakh, and has authored three groundbreaking books on pain and movement.
